Support Us

Donate

Financial support for Peoples Oakland's mission, vision, and members is always appreciated. The Board of Directors of Peoples Oakland is seeking donations of all sizes from foundations, corporations, and individuals to help us continue our groundbreaking work in aiding mental health consumers in achieving their recovery goals. Technology upgrades, the development of innovative programs to improve program participation, wellness center equipment, and capacity building are some of our current needs. Bequests and gifts of securities are also very welcome.

Volunteer

Peoples Oakland is always looking for dedicated, creative, people-oriented, community volunteers who have the interest, talents and time to share with our members and staff. Volunteers assist with daily programs activities, technology issues, fundraising events and serve on the Board of Directors.

Internships

Peoples Oakland has placements available each term for college students to complete their internships and/or practicum at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. We work with the various disciplines and departments such as social work, psychology, pharmacology and information technology.

Check with your academic program adviser to see if a Peoples Oakland academic placement is right for you. For more information, please contact:

  • Andrea Tempalski: andreat@peoplesoakland.org
