Special Events

Meditation & Recovery Group
Mon-Fri 10am-11am

Finding time to release your stress can help you learn to control your emotions. Every morning, we hold a 1-hour meditation center at the Peoples Oakland fitness center. Come join us to relax your body and to clear your mind.

Virtual Group
Mon-Fri 2pm-3pm

Holding group discussions and consultations is crucial for your recovery. To ensure that you have the time to regularly communicate about mental health issues, we are holding virtual group meetings on Zoom every day from 2-3pm. Join us at Zoom Meeting ID: 133 729 051 .

Open Gym
Mon-Fri 3pm-4pm

Regular exercise is vital for maintaining a healthy life! Our gym is open to all members from 3-4 pm every day. We have treadmills, weights, exercise benches, and more available for your workout.
